My History

My web design career journey began when I struggled to find my college's campus map on their website. The website mirrored the complex layout of the campus, making it difficult to navigate, especially on a phone. The frustrating experience of constantly zooming, swiping, and scrolling just to locate a simple map felt unnecessary. This lack of user-friendliness sparked my desire for building elegant and intuitive websites that prioritize user experience!
