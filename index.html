My History

My web design career journey began when I struggled to find my college's campus map on their website. The website mirrored the complex layout of the campus, making it difficult to navigate, especially on a phone. The frustrating experience of constantly zooming, swiping, and scrolling just to locate a simple map felt unnecessary. This lack of user-friendliness sparked my desire for building elegant and intuitive websites that prioritize user experience!

Why should you work with me?

One of my strongest skills I developed while working in luxury retail was listening to my clients needs and providing solutions. When they were torn on making a decision, I gave them my honest opinion but reminded them what their needs were. I carried this polished skill over into my web career and my clients couldn't be happier to know they are not just another invoice number. I take pride in my work and strive to make it personalized instead of another cookie cutter template bloated with distractions or information that take away from the goal of your website.
